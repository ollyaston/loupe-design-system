# Steppers

Step-by-step process components for workflows, multi-step forms, and guided user experiences. These components help users navigate through complex processes with clear progress indication.

## What are Stepper Components?

Stepper components provide visual progress indicators and navigation for multi-step processes. They offer:

- **Progress indication** - Clear visual feedback on current position
- **Process guidance** - Step-by-step user experience
- **Navigation control** - Easy movement between steps
- **Status communication** - Visual representation of step states

## Component Categories

### Step Navigation

- **Stepper** - Main stepper component with step navigation
- **BigStep** - Large step indicators for major milestones
- **CollapsibleStep** - Expandable step details and information
- **StepBadge** - Step status indicators and progress badges

## Usage Guidelines

### When to use Stepper Components

- For multi-step forms and wizards
- When guiding users through complex processes
- For onboarding and setup flows
- When showing progress through workflows
- For step-by-step tutorials and guides

### Best Practices

- Keep steps focused and manageable
- Provide clear step titles and descriptions
- Allow users to go back and edit previous steps
- Show progress and completion status
- Test with different step counts and content lengths
