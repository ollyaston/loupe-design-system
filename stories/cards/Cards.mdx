# Cards

Card components for content grouping, workflow visualization, and structured information display. These components provide flexible containers for organizing related content and actions.

## What are Card Components?

Card components are flexible containers that group related content and actions together. They provide:

- **Content organization** - Logical grouping of related information
- **Visual hierarchy** - Clear separation between different content areas
- **Interactive elements** - Clickable cards with hover states and actions
- **Responsive design** - Adaptive layouts for different screen sizes

## Component Categories

### Basic Cards

- **Card** - Standard card container with header, content, and footer

## Usage Guidelines

### When to use Card Components

- For grouping related content and actions
- When displaying structured information
- For workflow and process visualization
- When creating dashboard layouts
- For product or feature showcases

### Best Practices

- Keep card content focused and related
- Use consistent spacing and alignment
- Provide clear visual hierarchy
- Ensure cards are accessible and keyboard navigable
- Test with different content lengths

### Grid Layout

### Clickable Cards

## Card States

### Loading State
